We design for greatness

Here at Serpent Studios, we are passionate about great work. There are so many neglected projects, forgotten ideas and wasted potential, and we’re here to change that. We’re here to give a face and voice to your project, startup company and idea. Branding is an investment, and for your passion project, it’s an investment on yourself. You and your dreams are your most powerful assets, and we recognize that - it’s why we’re here to help!

Week 1: The Briefing

This is step one in the branding process. Before we can figure out what you need and what looks good, first we need to figure out who you are and what you’re all about. That’s what this step is for. We’ll start off strong with a meeting discussing your mission, vision, your ambitions, target audience, and more - all so we can both have a clear idea of why you do what you do, and how we can help you accomplish that.

Week 2: The Translation

This step is all about translation. This is where we discuss what we already know about you and your company or project, and then look at how that would apply in a visual (branding) context. We go through a moodboard with you and help you figure out what it is that really speaks to your business or project, and what really speaks to the audience you’re trying to communicate to. This step is the key for our designers to translate your words into images, colors, fonts, and more.

Week 3: The Revisions

Now here’s the fun part. After we’ve figured out who you are and what that looks like, this is our presentation and revision week. This is the week where we present stylescapes, logos, and more based on the information we’ve already spent the past couple weeks discussing. This is the part where we show you fancy logos and fonts that help you blend in into your market, but stand out next to your competition.

Week 4: The Finalization

Now that we’ve figured out what works and doesn’t work for your company or project, we enter into the finalization phase. This phase is where we settle on what works for you and what doesn’t, or we make revisions to ensure that every asset, color and even word matches the exact message you’re trying to send to your customers.
