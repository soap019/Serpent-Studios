Who We Are

We Are Serpent Studios

We are a design studio based in Alpharetta, Georgia, dedicated to helping people create the business of their dream. In order to do that, we deliver stunning brands, photos, deliverables, leadership training and other supportive services to assist passionate entrepreneurs and dreamers. We believe that good work comes from those who are continuously inspired and pursuing their ambitious dreams. We are here to help bridge that gap between ambition and reality, whether it’s delivering a brand guide, marketing plan and more, or a photoshoot for your music album.
