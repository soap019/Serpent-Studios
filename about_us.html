We Are Serpent Studios

Based in Alpharetta, GA, we are dedicated to helping people create the business of their dream. In order to do that, we deliver stunning brands, photos, deliverables, leadership training and other supportive services to assist entrepreneurs and dreamers who are passionate about their own work. Good work comes from those who are continuously inspired and pursuing their ambitious dreams, and this is why we are inspired to do what we do everyday. We are here to help bridge that gap between ambition and reality, whether it’s delivering a brand guide, marketing plan and more, or a photoshoot for your music album.

Graphic Design

From simple logo designs to complicated graphics, find what you’re looking for in terms of graphical art.

Photography

From event photography to portraits - a way to capture precious memories and moments through the lens of a professional photographer.

Branding

Get a hand in building your business from the ground up with deliverable logos, a brand guide, and more.
