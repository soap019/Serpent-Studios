CircuitRunners

CircuitRunners Robotics was a robotics nonprofit located in Marietta, Georgia focused on helping teach students about science, technology, engineering, and math in an applicable way. They are one of the oldest teams in Georgia, and were looking to rebrand into something more modern and unique than their competitors. Because of the team's long history though, they opted to keep many elements that were instrumental in their previous years while still showing off a new persona and unique style.

Inspired by the popularity of companies like AirBnB, Lyft, and UberEats, PCH was inspired to have a modern and friendly brand that easily conveys an "at home service" ideal.

Reset Robotics

Reset Robotics was a nonprofit dedicated to teaching students about STEM and inspiring students to be innovators in their community. Reset wanted to come out of the gate as a very impressionable brand with a modern, yet innovative mindset, focusing heavily on ways to impress with stunning visuals and graphics. Minimalism also played an integral part of this brand's persona with the idea that "less is more" when it comes to innovation and inspiration.

GeorgiaFIRST Robotics

GeorgiaFIRST Robotics is a local nonprofit organization dedicated to helping spread FIRST's (For Inspiration of Science and Technology) mission across the globe. FIRST Robotics is the world's largest youth serving nonprofit advancing STEM education across the world, and currently has teams and competitions in over 80 countries. Here in Georgia, there are hundreds of teams across multiple grade levels that compete and volunteer at GeorgiaFIRST's events, all of them dedicated to the mission of furthering STEM education and access.

The

Drop

The Drop is a startup company that prioritizes helping connect local entrepreneurs to potential clients looking for their services in an area they may be unfamiliar with. For this project, The Drop was looking for a brand that gave a professional impression, while still remaining connected to it's tech - savvy target audience with colorful splashes of color. This contrast with desaturated and black and white pictures / assets helped keep the confusion and chaos in the brand minimal, while still capturing the attention it needed.
